Welcome to my research website. # Introduction

Goal of Study

Complex diseases, like cancer, are typically multi-pathogenesis on genomic aspect. Different treatment could be developed accordingly if the subtype of disease can be correctly specified. In this study, we try to develop a new method to build similarity networks between patients based on the integrated multi-omics data, and using spectral clustering for further subtypes identification.
